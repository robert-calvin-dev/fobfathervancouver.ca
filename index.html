Why We Started Our Business

In downtown Vancouver, many residents are paying ridiculous prices for key fob copying through apartment management companies and strata offices. These inflated rates can easily cost residents hundreds of dollars for something that should be affordable and accessible. We created FobFather Vancouver to stop this cycle and offer a smarter, more cost-effective solution.


We believe that every resident in downtown Vancouver — whether in the West End, Yaletown, or Coal Harbour — deserves a fair price for key fob duplication. That’s why FobFather exists: to make secure building access affordable for everyone while keeping convenience and trust at the center of what we do.
